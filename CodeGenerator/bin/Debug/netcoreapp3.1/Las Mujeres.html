Las Mujeres

We're a Latina/women's empowerment organization that focuses on personal, professional and communal betterment, creating a space of positive support, garnering allies, and giving back to our communities. Las Mujeres PSU addresses the needs of Chicano/Hispanic/Latina women on campus and the community through community partners and community awareness.
