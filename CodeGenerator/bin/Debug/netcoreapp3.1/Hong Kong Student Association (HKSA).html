Hong Kong Student Association (HKSA)

HKSA is a student-run, multicultural student group dedicated to bringing Hong Kong culture to PSU We strive to expand PSU community's knowledge of cultural adjustment, education, and social community. Our group offers educational workshops, volunteer work and a welcoming space where members can share their unique skills and make friends.
