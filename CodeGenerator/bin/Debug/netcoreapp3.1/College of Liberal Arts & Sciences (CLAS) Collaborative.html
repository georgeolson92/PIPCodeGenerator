College of Liberal Arts & Sciences (CLAS) Collaborative

The objective of CLAS Collaborative is to provide PSU students additional support and resources to contribute to their professional and academic development through collaboration within the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences (CLAS). This is achieved by offering teaching education, discipline-specific workshops, interdisciplinary collaboration, and funding for academic advancement.
