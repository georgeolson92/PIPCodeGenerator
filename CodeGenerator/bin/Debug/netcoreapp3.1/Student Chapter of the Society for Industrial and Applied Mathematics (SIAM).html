Student Chapter of the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM)

SIAM has Student Chapters around the world for students interested in computational and applied mathematics. The PSU Chapter was formed to organize activities for students in science, engineering, math, and statistics. We organize resources for students such as seminar talks, workshops in the computer lab, field trips, and more!
