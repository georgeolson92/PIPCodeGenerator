Omega Psi Phi

Omega Psi Phi Fraternity Incorporated was founded November 17, 1911. The fraternity strives to incorporate brotherhood through scholarship, perseverance, manhood. uplifting our fellow man and the community. Also increasing community and awareness to African American men who have traditionally been oppressed, marginalized and had our voices silenced throughout American history.
