Geospatial Information Society (GIS) (Formerly ASPRS)

We provide opportunities for students to learn about applied GIS, photogrammetry, and remote sensing, and to meet and collaborate with professionals in those fields.We organize professional colloquia, field trips, symposiums, and career development workshops. Students also have the chance to work on data sets and problems from various institutions.
