Zeta Phi Beta

Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Incorporated was founded on the principles of scholarship, service, sisterhood and finer womanhood. The Eta Phi Chapter of Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Incorporated was chartered on March 27, 2017, the first Black Greek Letter Sorority to have a single charter on the campus of PSU.
